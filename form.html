Important Notice for New Accounts

For New Memberships i.e. for those who have already completed AA1, Do Not set-up a new account - you are already in our database.  If you have forgotten your User Name and Password, email AFCI at info@afcinstitute.com and we will send you your User Name and a Temporary Password to use.  This will ensure your education history remains in the same account.
